## Splash into Bubbles

This study presents an interactive device based on a progressive sensory fading strategy, designed to guide users into sleep. The device provides real-time feedback on respiratory signal changes through the sequential fading of visual, auditory, and haptic cues across three sensory channels, simulating the physiological rhythm of gradual sensory shutdown during sleep onset. By externalizing internal physiological signals, it reduces pre-sleep multidimensional arousal levels — cognitive arousal, physiological arousal, and emotional arousal — thereby facilitating sleep onset. 

本研究提出一种基于渐进式感官消退策略的交互装置，旨在引导使用者进入睡眠状态。该装置通过视觉、听觉与触觉三种感官通道的线索依次淡出，实时反馈呼吸信号的变化，以模拟入睡过程中感官逐步关闭的生理节律。同时，装置将内在生理信号外化呈现，以此降低睡前的多维度唤醒水平（认知唤醒、生理唤醒与情绪唤醒），从而促进睡眠 onset 的发生。

## Echoes of the Body

A multisensory biofeedback system that turns real-time physiological signals (EEG, heart rate, respiration, EDA) into synchronized visual, auditory, and haptic feedback, easing users toward a calm, positive pre-sleep state. Rooted in the hyperarousal model and interoceptive awareness theory, it leverages cardiorespiratory coordination to guide physiological down-regulation. 

一套多感官生物反馈系统，将实时生理信号（EEG、心率、呼吸、皮肤电）同步转化为视觉、听觉与触觉反馈，引导用户进入平静、积极的睡前状态。基于过度觉醒模型与内感受知觉理论，通过心肺耦合等生理指标引导生理下调。发表于 ACM ICHEC 2025 与 DIS 2026。

## EmoTun

EmoTun is a three-layer musical co-creation system for expressive arts therapy. Users control melody through hand gestures, EEG-driven emotion classification modulates timbre, and an LLM generates harmonic loop accompaniment, lowering the barrier to expressive, human–AI musical co-creation. Its core concept is physiologically-anchored scaffolding: instead of replacing human creativity, AI support is grounded in real-time bodily signals. Suitable for bedside improvisation and personal meditation.

EmoTun 是一个面向表达性艺术治疗的三层音乐共创系统：用户通过手势控制旋律，EEG 情绪分类调节音色，LLM 生成和声循环伴奏，降低了人机音乐共创的表达门槛。其核心理念为生理锚定的脚手架——AI 的支持不是替代人类创造力，而是建立在实时身体信号之上。适用于枕边即兴与个人冥想。

## Sleep Whisper

A smart napping system that tracks sleep stages via EEG to wake users at the optimal moment, preventing sleep inertia. Upon waking, generative AI reconstructs brain signals into personalized imagery and music, enabling dream recording and replay. Survey data showed over 70% of users are interested in recording and exploring their dreams.

一款智能午睡系统，通过 EEG 追踪睡眠阶段并在最佳时机唤醒用户以避免睡眠惯性；午睡结束后利用生成式 AI 将脑信号重构为个性化图像与音乐，实现梦境的记录与再现。调研显示超过 70% 的用户有兴趣记录并探索自己的梦境。

## EatTogether 2050

A speculative dining-and-social system for 2050 Shenzhen, exploring personalized food experiences and remote emotional connection through AI, 3D printing, and AR/VR. Features include gamified mystery-box bento selection, immersive cooking lessons that replicate memory-laden flavors, and holographic remote dining. Exhibited at Edible Urban Futures, Melbourne School of Design.

一套面向 2050 年深圳的推测性餐饮社交系统，在高流动、弱厨房的城市语境下，通过 AI、3D 打印与 AR/VR 等技术重建饮食的社交与情感价值，包括外卖盲盒游戏化选餐、沉浸式烹饪教学复刻记忆味道，以及远程全息共餐体验。于墨尔本设计学院 Edible Urban Futures 展览展出。
